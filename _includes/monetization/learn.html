Exactly What You'll Learn.

People no longer care about extreme false promises.

You don't have to sell your soul by promising people they will make 1 million dollars in 30 days.

People want clear education from someone who is 1 to 2 steps ahead of them.

That's what makes for a great product and learning experience that gets results.

In other words, you don't need a ton of experience to make this work.

Think about it.

Someone who hires a personal trainer probably doesn't want to be trained by a bodybuilder.

They will be insecure the entire time.

They want to be trained by someone who is close to their level, even if that trainer is overweight and doesn't eat the perfect diet.
