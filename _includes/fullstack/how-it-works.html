How It Works

A simple 5-week structure designed for clarity, action, and results.

Start

Week 1: Foundation

Clarify your niche, your message, and why you’re writing. Set the stage for consistent, impactful content.

Week 2: Persuasive Writing

Learn frameworks like PASO, PASTOR, and 10 Commandments Of Engagement to write content that gets attention and drives action.

Week 3: Build Your Content Stack

Plan content pillars and structure your digital ecosystem. Create authority-building formats like carousels and essays.

Week 4: Automation & Swipe File

Use templates, swipe files, and tools to create 30–60 days of content in 4 hours.

Week 5: Launch Your Ecosystem

Connect your brand, your content, and your products into one flow. Start growing and monetizing on autopilot.
