You're reading this because you have skills, interests, and expertise you want to turn into your own work.

You're a beginner who wants a business that works with any skill.

You're a creative who doesn't want to rely on publishers, record labels, or freelance platforms to find work.

You're a founder who realized the power of social media to amplify your presence and add to your business.

In other words, you need an audience.

That's business and creative work 101. You can go the paid ads route for an audience. You can go the SEO route for an audience. But if you want a route that's free, accessible, and high-leverage... you have one option.

Write online. Build a profitable brand.

Audience building comes down to 6 things:

  • Testing ideas and structures on social media.
  • Having a catalyst strategy for new posts.
  • Turning best ideas into threads & newsletters.
  • Repurposing your writing to other platforms.
  • Taking advantage of exponential events.
  • Sending everyone to your newsletter.

These are the only things you need to focus on every morning.

When done well, it should take no longer than 1-2 hours a day.
